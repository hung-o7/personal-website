Ingredients

·Apples! 7 Granny Smith Apples

·Warm Butter and flour

·Sugar, both white and brown

·Pie Crust

·Cinnamon

Directions

·Cut apples into thin slices

·Lightly cover Apple Slices in Cinnamon

·mix both sugars into a water mixture (2 cups)

·Add butter and flour to a pot

·Add water mixture to the pot, then put on low heat until syrup thick

·Add Apple Slices to in a circular patern around the crust

·Add the sugar sauce on top of the pie

·Bake at 370F for 50 minutes or until golden brown in 5-minute intervals
